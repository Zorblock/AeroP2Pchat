Aero P2P Chat Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Aero P2P Chat is a peer-to-peer chat client. We do not require an account and do not sell or use personal data for advertising or analytics.

Data handled by the app

Your app settings, profile details, contacts, and chat history are stored locally in your browser or installed app. Messages, voice calls, camera streams, and screen shares are sent directly to the peer you choose to connect with. They are not stored by Aero P2P Chat.

A PeerJS signaling service is used only to establish a connection between peers. The service is not used to store chat messages or call media.

Chrome extension permissions

  • Storage: saves your app settings and local chat data on your device.
  • Notifications: shows notifications for messages and calls when you allow them.
  • Tabs: opens or focuses the Aero P2P Chat extension tab when you click the extension icon.

The extension does not read webpage content, browsing history, passwords, payment data, or unrelated browser data.

Your choices

You can delete chats and contacts through the app. Removing the extension deletes its locally stored extension data from Chrome. You can also revoke notification permissions in Chrome at any time.

Contact and support

For questions or support, open an issue at github.com/Zorblock/AeroP2Pchat/issues.
